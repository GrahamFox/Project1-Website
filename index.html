About Us

Craic Ops Gaming is a website that aims to bring gamers across the country together so they can communicate with each other, find friends to play with and organise online games and tournaments. In Ireland, there is little or no community based organisations for gamers. A website dedicated to this, would ensure the growth of the community and introduce more people of all ages as it expands. The ultimate aim is to organise and host a convention in Dublin, with gamers from all over the country attending to participate in tournaments, competitions and perhaps to meet the people who are involved in developing the games that they love.
